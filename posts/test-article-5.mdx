## The Root Problem of Modern Computing

Computing has become centralized around a handful of massive tech companies that act as digital gatekeepers. From web browsers that track your mouse movements to cloud services that analyze your documents, we are losing our digital autonomy.

## Breaking Free From Gatekeepers

RebelRoot was founded by independent developers to solve this root problem. We believe high-quality, fully open-source alternatives are the only way to re-establish agency:

- **Auditable Pipelines**: You don't have to trust our word; you can audit the source code yourself.
- **Local-First Design**: Applications should run locally and store data on your physical device, not in the cloud.
- **Interoperability**: Using open standards ensures that your data remains yours even if you switch apps.

By funding independent development groups directly instead of relying on venture-backed corporations, users can ensure that the software they use serves *them*, and not marketing algorithms.
